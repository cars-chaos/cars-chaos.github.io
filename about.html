M'Aiken Magic Robotics Team

About

M’Aiken Magic Robotics is an exciting program that has brought immersive STEAM Education opportunities to high school students in the CSRA since 2003. Our team competes in the FIRST Robotics Competition challenge, known as the ultimate sport for the mind. Under strict rules and on a 6-week timeline, teams of students are challenged to design, build and program 135 LB industrial-sized robots to play difficult sports inspired games. It’s as close to real-world engineering as a high school student can get.

⠀

One important focus of our program is to ensure our students are getting the appropriate professional development opportunities. We are not just focused on technical education, but we also elevate students’ soft skills such as leadership, communication, and conflict resolution. Last year we invited a group of automation engineers from the Savannah River National Lab for a Q&A session to develop our student’s technical communication skills.

⠀

We also have our students represent our organization at local community STEM education events like the D.I.G. STEM festival and SEED at USC Aiken where they have spoken with over 3,000 members of our community. We also select student leadership using a real-world job application process, where we help students to submit high quality cover letters and resumes and coach them through interviews.
